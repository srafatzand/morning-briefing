## Source Rules

**Geopolitics & Iran — restricted to:**
Reuters, Al Jazeera, NYT, WaPo, BBC, CBC, The Economist.
Geopolitics must represent both Western and non-Western perspectives where available.

**AI & Tech, Biotech & Medicine — flexible, prefer:**
Reuters, NYT, WaPo, BBC, The Economist, Nature, Science, JAMA, NEJM.

**All topics — prohibited:**
Twitter/X, Reddit, Substack, personal blogs, unaccredited/anonymous sources.
